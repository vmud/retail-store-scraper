>
> **Last Updated**: February 2025
>
> **Scope**: National chains with physical retail presence; excludes pure-play e-commerce without stores.

## Consumer Electronics Specialty Retailers

### Best Buy
- **Store Count**: ~1,000 US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (BBY)
- **CE Categories**: Full-line — TVs, computing, mobile, appliances, smart home, gaming, car electronics, pro audio
- **Notable Programs**: Totaltech membership, Geek Squad services, vendor shop-in-shops (Apple, Samsung, Sony, Microsoft, Google, Magnolia)
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Primary CE retail partner for most major brands; highest assisted selling volume

### Micro Center
- **Store Count**: 25+ locations (major metros only)
- **Ownership**: Private
- **CE Categories**: Computing-focused — components, DIY PC builds, laptops, peripherals, some mobile
- **Notable Programs**: In-store pickup, custom PC building services
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Enthusiast/prosumer audience; high-value computing sales

---

## Mass Merchandisers

### Walmart
- **Store Count**: ~4,700 US locations (Supercenters, Neighborhood Markets, Discount Stores)
- **Ownership**: Public (WMT)
- **CE Categories**: TVs, computing, mobile (carrier partnerships), tablets, gaming, accessories, small appliances
- **Notable Programs**: Walmart+ membership, extensive online/pickup integration
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Largest physical retail footprint; value-focused consumer; high volume

### Target
- **Store Count**: ~1,950 US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (TGT)
- **CE Categories**: TVs, computing, mobile, tablets, gaming, smart home, wearables
- **Notable Programs**: Apple shop-in-shops, Target Circle loyalty, same-day services
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Higher household income demographic; strong in mobile and Apple ecosystem

### Meijer
- **Store Count**: ~270 locations
- **Regional Footprint**: Michigan, Ohio, Indiana, Illinois, Kentucky, Wisconsin
- **Ownership**: Private (family-owned)
- **CE Categories**: TVs, computing, mobile, gaming, accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional mass merchant; Midwest coverage

### Fred Meyer
- **Store Count**: ~130 locations
- **Regional Footprint**: Pacific Northwest (OR, WA, ID, AK)
- **Ownership**: Kroger subsidiary
- **CE Categories**: Basic CE departments — TVs, computing, mobile accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional hypermarket; Kroger banner

---

## Warehouse Clubs

### Costco
- **Store Count**: ~600+ US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (COST)
- **CE Categories**: Premium TVs, laptops, tablets, mobile (carrier activations), cameras, appliances, smart home
- **Notable Programs**: Costco Concierge Services (tech support), extended warranties, membership model
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Premium product placement; high average transaction value; membership filters demographics

### Sam's Club
- **Store Count**: ~600 US locations
- **Ownership**: Walmart Inc.
- **CE Categories**: TVs, computing, mobile, tablets, gaming, appliances
- **Notable Programs**: Plus membership tiers, Scan & Go technology
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Value-focused warehouse club; overlaps with Walmart strategy

### BJ's Wholesale Club
- **Store Count**: ~240 locations
- **Regional Footprint**: Eastern US concentrated (Maine to Florida, westward to Ohio)
- **Ownership**: Public (BJ)
- **CE Categories**: TVs, computing, mobile, tablets, gaming
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional warehouse club; Eastern seaboard focus

---

## Wireless Carrier Retail

### Verizon
- **Store Count**: ~6,000+ (corporate + authorized retailers)
- **Ownership**: Public (VZ)
- **CE Categories**: Mobile devices, tablets, wearables, connected devices, smart home, accessories
- **Store Types**: Corporate stores, authorized retailers, kiosks
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Premium carrier positioning; strong accessory attach; corporate stores high-value

### AT&T
- **Store Count**: ~5,000+ (corporate + authorized retailers)
- **Ownership**: Public (T)
- **CE Categories**: Mobile devices, tablets, wearables, connected devices, accessories
- **Store Types**: Corporate stores, authorized retailers
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Large footprint; varied store quality between corporate/authorized

### T-Mobile
- **Store Count**: ~7,000+ (includes former Sprint locations)
- **Ownership**: Public (TMUS)
- **CE Categories**: Mobile devices, tablets, wearables, accessories
- **Store Types**: Corporate stores, authorized retailers, Metro by T-Mobile
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Largest store count post-Sprint merger; value-to-premium spectrum

### US Cellular
- **Store Count**: ~4,000+ (corporate + agents)
- **Regional Footprint**: Midwest, Northwest, portions of Mid-Atlantic
- **Ownership**: Public (USM)
- **CE Categories**: Mobile devices, tablets, accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional carrier; rural/suburban markets

## Office Supply Retailers

### Staples
- **Store Count**: ~1,000 US locations
- **Ownership**: Private (Sycamore Partners)
- **CE Categories**: Computing (laptops, desktops), printers, monitors, networking, mobile accessories, tablets
- **Notable Programs**: Business services focus, tech services
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: B2B/SMB customer base; computing and productivity focus

### Office Depot / OfficeMax
- **Store Count**: ~1,000 US locations (combined banners)
- **Ownership**: Public (ODP Corporation)
- **CE Categories**: Computing, printers, monitors, networking, accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Similar to Staples; consolidating footprint

## Home Improvement Retailers

### The Home Depot
- **Store Count**: ~2,000 US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (HD)
- **CE Categories**: Smart home (thermostats, security, lighting), small appliances, major appliances
- **Notable Programs**: Pro customer focus, installation services
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Smart home category leader; contractor/DIY audience

### Lowe's
- **Store Count**: ~1,700 US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (LOW)
- **CE Categories**: Smart home, small appliances, major appliances
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Similar to Home Depot; slightly more consumer vs. pro mix

### Menards
- **Store Count**: ~330 locations
- **Regional Footprint**: Midwest (14 states)
- **Ownership**: Private (Menard family)
- **CE Categories**: Surprisingly broad — TVs, computing basics, smart home, appliances
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional; broader CE than typical home improvement

## Department Stores

### Kohl's
- **Store Count**: ~1,100 US locations
- **Ownership**: Public (KSS)
- **CE Categories**: Growing category — wearables, small electronics, accessories; Amazon returns partnership
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Expanding CE presence; Amazon relationship notable

### Macy's
- **Store Count**: ~500 US locations (declining)
- **Ownership**: Public (M)
- **CE Categories**: Limited — tech accessories, headphones, small electronics
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Minimal CE focus; premium accessories only

### JCPenney
- **Store Count**: ~650 US locations
- **Ownership**: Private (Simon Property Group, Brookfield Asset Management)
- **CE Categories**: Minimal — basic electronics, accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Limited CE relevance

### Nordstrom
- **Store Count**: ~350 US locations (full-line + Rack)
- **Ownership**: Private (Nordstrom family + partners)
- **CE Categories**: Premium accessories — headphones, wearables, tech accessories
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Premium/luxury positioning; high-end accessories only

## Grocery Retailers with CE Departments

### Kroger
- **Store Count**: ~2,700 US locations (multiple banners)
- **Ownership**: Public (KR)
- **CE Categories**: Basic electronics sections — varies by banner and store size
- **Banners Include**: Kroger, Ralphs, Fred Meyer, Harris Teeter, Smith's, King Soopers, Fry's Food
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Varies widely; Fred Meyer most significant for CE

### Albertsons / Safeway
- **Store Count**: ~2,200 US locations
- **Ownership**: Albertsons Companies (merger with Kroger pending/blocked)
- **CE Categories**: Limited — basic accessories, prepaid phones
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Minimal CE focus

### H-E-B
- **Store Count**: ~400+ locations
- **Regional Footprint**: Texas, portions of Mexico
- **Ownership**: Private (Butt family)
- **CE Categories**: Electronics departments in larger stores
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Regional; Texas-specific programs

## Brand-Owned Retail

### Apple
- **Store Count**: 270+ US locations
- **Ownership**: Apple Inc. (Public - AAPL)
- **CE Categories**: Apple ecosystem — iPhone, iPad, Mac, Watch, accessories, services
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Controlled retail environment; brand-exclusive

### Samsung
- **Store Count**: Experience stores within Best Buy locations + standalone Samsung 837 (NYC)
- **Ownership**: Samsung Electronics
- **CE Categories**: Samsung ecosystem — mobile, TV, appliances, wearables
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Shop-in-shop model; Best Buy partnership primary

### Microsoft
- **Store Count**: Physical stores closed (June 2020); online + Experience Centers only
- **Ownership**: Microsoft Corporation (Public - MSFT)
- **CE Categories**: Surface, Xbox, accessories (through partner retail)
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Partner retail focus (Best Buy primary)

## Gaming & Entertainment

### GameStop
- **Store Count**: ~4,000 US locations (declining)
- **Ownership**: Public (GME)
- **CE Categories**: Video game hardware (consoles), software, accessories, collectibles, refurbished devices

## Specialty / Niche Retailers

### B&H Photo Video
- **Store Count**: 1 flagship (NYC) + national e-commerce
- **Ownership**: Private
- **CE Categories**: Full professional-grade — cameras, pro audio, video production, computing, consumer electronics
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Pro/enthusiast audience; high-value transactions; e-commerce dominant

### Adorama
- **Store Count**: 1 location (NYC) + national e-commerce
- **Ownership**: Private
- **CE Categories**: Similar to B&H — cameras, pro audio, video, computing
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Pro/enthusiast audience

### Guitar Center
- **Store Count**: ~300 US locations
- **Ownership**: Private (creditor-owned post-bankruptcy)
- **CE Categories**: Pro audio, recording equipment, music production technology
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Music/audio professional audience

### REI
- **Store Count**: ~180 US locations
- **Ownership**: Consumer cooperative
- **CE Categories**: Outdoor tech — GPS devices, fitness wearables, action cameras, solar chargers
- **Field Marketing Relevance**: Outdoor enthusiast audience; premium outdoor tech
